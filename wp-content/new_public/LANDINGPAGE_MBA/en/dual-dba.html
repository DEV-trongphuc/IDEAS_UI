RESEARCH PATHWAY

4 Stages
2.5 - 3 Years - 1 Thesis

The research pathway is scientifically designed and divided into 4 clear stages, helping candidates develop strategic research capabilities and outstandingly complete their Doctorate thesis.

I Stage 1: Competitive Advantage
  • About 12 Months
  • Attend 6 seminar sessions (2-3 hours each) along with 1-on-1 individual advisory sessions.
  • Draft the thesis proposal comprehensively.
II Stage 2: Advisor Selection
  • About 3 Months
  • Select faculty advisors from IDEAS or Estiam (fee applies).
  • Finalize the research proposal and research plan.
  • End of stage: Official research topic application.
III Stage 3: Research with Advisor (1-on-1)
  • About 15 - 18 Months
  • Research under direct supervision (1-on-1).
  • Submit periodic progress reports.
  • Complete the full thesis with a length of approximately 50,000 words (200–250 pages).
IV Stage 4: Graduation Thesis Defense
  • About 3 Months
  • Complete the thesis and defend it before the Thesis Defense Committee.
